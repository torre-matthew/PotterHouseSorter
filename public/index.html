Which Hogwarts House Does Your Family and Friends Belong In?

At the begining of every Hogwarts school year, new students are sorted into houses. A student's placement into a house is based on all the unique characteristics that make them who they are.

House Gryffindor
What Gryffindors are made of...close

The house of the brave, loyal, courageous, adventurous, daring and chivalrous. Those who stand up for others are typically Gryffindors. Brave-hearted is the most well-known Gryffindor characteristic, and Gryffindors are also known for having a lot of nerve.

"Do what is right" is the motto of this house.

House Slytherin
What Slytherins are made of...close

This is the house of the cunning, prideful, resourceful, ambitious, intelligent, and determined. Slytherin's love to be in charge and crave leadership.

"Do what is necessary" is the motto of this house.
